Integrate financial and maintenance asset management.
===========================

This module allows use the same Assets for maintenance and accounting purposes. Integration take in account assumption that maintenable asset is physical asset and must have depreciation. Also vice versa, if asset have depreciation then it must be maintenable. So, when you create asset for accounting, it automaticaly available for maintenance. And when you create asset for maintenance, it automaticaly available for finance. Keep one entity in one place for escape mistakes!
