== Manage the planned and commited costs of your project ==

Eficent brings you this module to better manage the planned and commited costs of your projects.

This module defines a new structure to record planned and commited costs of the projects and contracts.

== New planning journal ==

A planning journal is create. It is similar to the analytic journal, but used for planning purposes.

The planning analytic journals can be configured.

A planning analytic journal lines object is created, with the exceptions of referencing to the planning analytic journal instead of the analytic journal, and considering that the general account is not a required entry.

The new object is visible as a separate entity, accesible from the Accounting area, with the corresponding search, tree, form views.

New analytic account planning attributes: cumulated planned costs, cummulated planned earnings and cumulated balance. The attributes are calculated based on the planning analytic journal lines.

The new attributes are visible on the following views: Analytic account forms: cumulated planned costs, cumulated planned earnings and cumulated balance; Budget positions: cumulated planned costs

A report exists to create a PDF with the corresponding journal entries.

== New commitments journal ==

A commitment journal is created. It is similar to the analytic journal, but used to record costs that have been commited to the project but are not yet actual expenses.

The commitment analytic journals can be configured.

A commitment analytic journal lines object is created, with the exceptions of referencing to the planning analytic journal instead of the analytic journal, and considering that the general account is not a required entry.

The new object is visible as a separate entity, accesible from the Accounting area, with the corresponding search, tree, form views.

New analytic account planning attributes: cumulated commitment costs, cummulated commitment earnings and cumulated balance. The attributes are calculated based on the commitment analytic journal lines.

The new attributes are visible on the following views: Analytic account forms: cumulated commited costs, cumulated commited earnings and cumulated commited balance; Budget positions: cumulated commited costs

A report exists to create a PDF with the corresponding journal entries.
