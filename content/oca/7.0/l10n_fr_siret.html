  • Description

This module lets users keep track of the companies' unique identification numbers from the official SIRENE registry in France: SIRET, SIREN and NIC. These numbers identify each company and their subsidiaries, and are often required for administrative tasks.

At the top of the Partner form, users will be able to enter the SIREN and NIC numbers, and the SIRET number will be calculated automatically. The last digits of the SIREN and NIC are control keys: OpenERP will check their validity when partners are recorded.
