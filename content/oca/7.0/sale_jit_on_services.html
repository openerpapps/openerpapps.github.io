Sale Service Just In Time

When you make a SO with products and services, the workflow of the SO will not reach the state done unless you deliver all products and procurements linked to service products are done.

Usually, when the MRP runs, it marks procurements of services' lines as done. But, you may want to mark them as done like if you were using the mrp_jit module.

This module provide that feature: It bring the behavior of the mrp_jit module but only on services products.
